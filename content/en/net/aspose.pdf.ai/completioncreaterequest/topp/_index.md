## CompletionCreateRequest.TopP property {#completioncreaterequesttopp-property}

Gets or sets an alternative to sampling with temperature, called nucleus sampling, where the model considers the results of the tokens with top_p probability mass. So 0.1 means only the tokens comprising the top 10% probability mass are considered.
